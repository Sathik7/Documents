About Us

A FEW WORD ABOUT US

Drawing inspiration from the South Indian city of spices, Calicut, Calicut Chef Restaurant offers a wholesome, multi-cuisine dining experience for families and individuals. At Calicut Chef Restaurant, we offer a one-of-a-kind dining experience, where we bring the flavors of India to the Middle East! We are registered under the ISO 22000:2018 - Food Safety Management System (FSMS) Standard. We believe that food should be a journey and that every dish has a story. Our multi-cuisine menu offers authentic Kerala cuisine, along with a hearty blend of fusion food, all of which are expertly plated by our talented chefs. Every single dish on our menu is bursting with flavor and cooked to perfection. Our cuisine offers an alluring atmosphere for all occasions, be it solo dining, family gathering, or business lunch. We believe that dining is more than just a meal - it is an opportunity to escape the daily grind, to connect with loved ones, and to create memories that will last a lifetime. Experience Calicut Chef Restaurant for a culinary delight that you will never forget!
